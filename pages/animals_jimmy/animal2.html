    Fun Facts!

    1) A lions roar can be heard up to 3 miles away

    2) In order for a new male to join the pride, they have to fight the ones already in it. The new male is either successful or driven off.

    3) Female lions raise the cubs and are also the primary hunters

    About!

    Lions are one of the most social felines. They live in groups called prides, which comrpises of several individuals -from 3 to up to 30 or more! Lions and lionesse have different roles in the pride. Lionesse are responsible for hunting and rearing the cubs. Lions guard the cubs while the lionesse hunts. Adult male lions are much larger than females and have impressive mane of hair around their neck.
