    Size & Weight

    Life expectancy: 25 years | Age of maturity: 3 to 6 years

    Height: 4.2 to 5 feet

    Weight: 550 to 990 pounds | Weight at birth: 55 to 88 pounds

    Fun Facts!

    1) A zebra's stripes are like human fingerprints. Each zebra has its own unique pattern

    2) White-colored stripes are cooler than their black ones

    3) Zebras can sleep standing up

    About!

    Zebras are equids, members of the horse family. They have excellent hearing and eyesight and can run at speeds of up to 35 miles per hour. Like horses, they also have a very powerful kick that can cause serious injury. zebras are herbivores and feed by grazing on grasses and other plants. Zebras are social herd animals, living in family groups. During certain times of the year, these groups gather together to form loosely associated herds. Zebras communicate with one another with facial expressions and sounds.
